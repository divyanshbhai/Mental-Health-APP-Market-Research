The Mano Mitra Solution

Mano Mitra addresses the core problems through four interconnected pillars. Click on each pillar to learn more about its features and the science behind it.

ü§ñ AI-Guided First-Aid

Provides immediate, 24/7, confidential support using evidence-based techniques. The bot acts as a first line of defense, offering coping strategies and escalating to human support when necessary.

  • Role-play therapy bot: Utilizes CBT & DBT frameworks to reframe negative thoughts.
  • Sentiment + Voice Emotion Recognition: Analyzes text and tone to understand the user's emotional state.
  • Mood Logging: Tracks emotional patterns through natural conversation.
